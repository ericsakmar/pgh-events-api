Ron Jackson Trio (NYC)

  • Wednesday, March 9, 2022
  • 6:00 PM 10:00 PM 18:00 22:00
  • Con Alma (map)
  • Google Calendar ICS

Ron Jackson- 7 String Guitar

Kyle Koehler-Hammond Organ

Corey Rawls- Drums

Ron Jackson's, "Whatever's Open" Tour with a night of Big City Jazz! 

Celebrated seven-string jazz guitarist Ron Jackson and his trio will perform songs from his new album, “Standards and My Songs” Ron's second recording as a seven-string guitarist and a follow-up to “Standards and Other Songs”, which was critically acclaimed and a 2019 JazzWeek Top 100 album! Like its’ predecessor, ...My Songs is comprised of well-known standards as well as pop and R&B songs reimagined as jazz. But this time around the album also features Ron's original compositions, which encompass bebop, swing, Latin, and modern jazz styles.

Standards and My Songs, “this cat is right on the money, right in the pocket and right on top of all the other clichés where bad means good.”-Chris Spector, Midwest Record 

Ron will present an exciting evening of jazz for all music lovers to enjoy. Audience members will be thoroughly entertained and feel like they are part of the show. 

Ron Jackson- 7 String Guitar

Kyle Koehler-Hammond Organ

Corey Rawls- Drums


This performance is made possible with the support of Jazz Road, a national initiative of South Arts, which is funded by the Doris Duke Charitable Foundation with additional support from The

Andrew W. Mellon Foundation.
