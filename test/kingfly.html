grab a drink. grab a seat.

We are proud to host local and national acts ranging from jazz, to singer/songwriters, comedians, spoken word, and much more. Join us in the Lounge, Loft our outdoor Courtyard for unforgettable live performances in a truly intimate setting.

 

Upcoming events.

Each week introduces diverse entertainment options in the unique setting of our beautifully-restored carriage house. Check our calendar and mark yours for the very best in live performances. For ticketed events, make sure to get your seat secured by paying in advance. We have multiple sell-out performances every month, so follow us on Facebook for upcoming show announcements.
